Concept Writing & Art

Overview

Concept and narrative design for Echoes Between, featuring story maps, world-building, and concept art for characters and environments on a tidally locked planet. Shortened summaries for portfolio purposes.

My Role

I developed all concept art, narrative planning, and story structure for this project, including character design, environmental concepts, and more.

Goals

The goals of this project were to create a cohesive fictional world suited for a video game. I wanted to design unique characters with depth, and who fit into the game world. Alongside this, I had to decide on a title, a detailed story map, and important gameplay elements. Overall, I aimed to design a realistic and intriguing video game concept.

Concept

On a planet split between darkness and light, a growing imbalance threatens to destroy it all. Journey across the fractured realms, uncovering forgotten truths, in order to mend the divide.

Echoes Between follows Nyra Orlith, a scout from the shadowed region of Umbraeth. Haunted by visions of collapse and driven by her severed connection to the spirit realm, Nyra must navigate dangerous terrains and divided societies to uncover the source of the imbalance. Her path crosses with Aeris Lucent, a determined agriculturist from Ignisca, the sunlit side of the planet. Together, they must uncover ancient secrets and confront their societies’ generational divide—or their homelands might not make it.

Brief Character Descriptions

Nyra Orlith — Protagonist (Umbraeth)

Nyra is a half-blind scout from the shadowed realm of Umbraeth, known for her heightened senses and bioluminescent clan markings. Stoic but deeply empathetic, she begins the story severed from her spiritual heritage after a childhood trauma. As visions of an ecological collapse emerge, Nyra embarks on a journey of self-discovery, determined to uncover the truth behind the imbalance affecting her world.

Aeris Lucent — Protagonist (Ignisca)

Aeris is an agriculturist from the sunlit region of Ignisca, energetic but grounded with a strong bond to nature. Skilled in cultivating flora and understanding wildlife, she becomes instrumental in diagnosing the planet’s environmental decline. When her path intertwines with Nyra’s, Aeris helps bridge the divide between their cultures while facing the crisis threatening both realms.

Brief Worldbuilding

Umbraeth

The perpetually dark side of Kairis, Umbraeth features towering forests, bioluminescent flora, and harsh terrain including mountains, ravines, and caves. The people are resilient and spiritual, valuing stealth and intuition. Many have heightened senses or levels of blindness, reflecting their adaptation to the environment. Clans are loosely organized, with family leadership playing a central role. Fauna have adapted to the darkness as well, with many hidden, silent hunters.

Ignisca

Bathed in constant sunlight, Ignisca is diverse, with fertile forests, plains, gardens, rivers, and arid zones. Its people embrace community, artistry, and agriculture, with leadership guided by councils. Citizens have darker skin and adaptations to the sun, while fauna are resilient, often with protective features against the heat. Architecture and culture emphasize open, vibrant, and uplifting spaces.

Virelia

The twilight border between Umbraeth and Ignisca blends elements of both ecosystems. Transitional landscapes include mixed flora and fauna, old ruins, and new settlements reflecting tentative peace. Cultural and linguistic exchanges occur here, serving as a bridge between the two regions after a long, devastating divide.

Brief Narrative Planning

Chapter 1: Nyra experiences a vision of ecological collapse and investigates her clan’s surroundings, discovering signs of decay. She uncovers evidence pointing toward Ignisca, setting her journey into motion.

Chapter 2: Introduces Aeris and the dual-player gameplay. Together, they explore Ignisca, uncover past alliances, and learn about Arion and Lyria’s role in history.

Nyra and Aeris investigate the twilight zone, facing environmental challenges while learning the root of the divide.

The girls reach the spirit core and attempt to restore balance, with multiple outcomes depending on player actions, showing consequences for the planet and the realms.

Gameplay Features

  • Dual-Protagonists
  • Dynamic World (environment changes based on imbalance)
  • Exploration/Survival
  • Open World
  • Choice-Based Narrative
  • Puzzles

Conclusion

This project strengthened my ability to combine visual development with narrative design. I learned how to build a unique world that feels culturally and environmentally coherent, while designing characters and story structures rooted in that world. Moving forwards, I'd expand the story map into a full narrative script and develop more concept art.
