Blush & Bloom Mobile Prototype

Overview

A mobile store prototype for Blush & Bloom, a plant shop with a fun green and pink palette. Built in Figma with interactive product selection and checkout flows.

My Role

I designed the prototype, including the UI layout, reusable components, color scheme, and interaction flow. The logo was created utilizing FontAwesome's plant icon, and I used additional FontAwesome icons throughout the design. Product images were sourced from copyright-free image sites.

Goals

The goal of this project was to create a mobile prototype for a made-up company. The prototype needed to showcase key features of an e-commerce app, including product browsing, selection, and checkout processes. I aimed to design an intuitive, aesthetic interface for a fictional plant shop called Blush & Bloom. I wanted to use a color scheme that reflected the brand's potential identity.

Concept

Blush & Bloom is an online plant shop selling a variety of houseplants and plant care accessories. My concept was intended to appeal to a young adult audience interested in gardening, especially those new to plant care. With this in mind, I decided to create a fun and inviting atmosphere using a green and pink color palette, with a hint of brown to represent soil. I designed the UI to be clean and easily-comprehensible for users, with clear navigation flows.
