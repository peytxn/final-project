About Me

Hi, I'm Peyton Blanchard, a designer and developer who enjoys creating intuitive and engaging digital experiences. I'm currently in East Lansing, MI, earning a dual B.A. in Information Science and Game Design & Development. These programs let me blend problem-solving, visual design, and storytelling across both web and game development.

I'm particularly interested in UI and UX design, as well as how small details influence how people feel when using a product. Regardless of the medium, I'm focused on clarity, flow, and aesthetics.

Outside of my projects, I enjoy reading, playing video games, and exploring new content. They're great sources of inspiration!

If you'd like to collaborate or converse, please don't hesitate to reach out. I'm always excited to connect with people!
